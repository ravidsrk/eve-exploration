# Refund eligibility policy

Apply these rules in order:

1. **Digital goods** (software, e-books, gift cards): non-refundable once delivered. No exceptions.
2. **Physical goods, unopened**: full refund within **30 days** of delivery, no fee.
3. **Physical goods, opened — electronics**: refundable within **30 days**, but a **15% restocking
   fee** is deducted.
4. **Physical goods, opened — non-electronics**: refundable within **30 days**, **20% restocking
   fee**.
5. **Anything past 30 days**: not refundable (offer store credit at 50% as a courtesy).

Always state the eligibility decision and the exact fee (if any).
